About Us

Cyc-soul is a non profit organization that has made it its goal to provide shoes to the ones in need. Cyc-soul acomplishes this by reciving donated shoes and if nessary fixig them up by replacing the old sole. We use local drives and the money we get for selling our products to buy and fix old shoes and give them to some of the many children in need all across the nation.

Our Vision

Giving aid to the people in need. A world where everyone is equal and has everything they need for living.

Our Mission

To provide the ones with little, with a little more.
